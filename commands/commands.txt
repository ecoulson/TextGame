move: Moves a player in a specified direction
look: Lists all items on the players tile
take: Takes an item from the tile your player is currently on
drop: Drops an item onto the tile your player is currently on
inventory: Lists all items in your players inventory
stats: Displays characters current stats
map: Displays the current map of the room the player is in
save: Saves the current world
exit: Saves and quits the current world and game
